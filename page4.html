Do Don't
Know the average prices and a few Sinhala words. Wear short clothes in religous places.
Try out a ride in Tuk Tuk, it's one of a kind. Get in a Tuk Tuk without discussing the price first.
Drink some of that famous Ceylon tea. Forget that PDA is not encouraged.
Try to eat rice and curry using your hand. Take uncivil pictures in holy places.
Visit some of the small islands around Sri Lanka. Think that traveling will be quick... quite the opposite.
Tour a tea estate in the hill country. Display tattoos of the Buddha (that's if you have any).
Go to an Ayurvedhic spa. Forget to take home some little souvenirs :)
Explore both cities and villages.
Have fun!
