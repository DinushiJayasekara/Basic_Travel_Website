Sri Lanka

A small island situated to the south of the Indian subcontinent, Sri Lanka is a country full of wonderful things to do and see at all times of the year.

Climate

This country has a warm climate throughout the year and 2 main monsoon seasons. The temperature is around 28-30Â°C but can be cooler in the hill country.

Food

Be prepared to go through a culinary journey flowing with spices when in Sri Lanka. Many Sri Lankans consider their staple food to be rice and curry.

People

Considered as some of the friendliest people in the world, it is very rarely that you won't find a Sri Lankan with a bright smile and a simple nod to say hello.
