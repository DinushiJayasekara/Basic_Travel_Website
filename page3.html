    Here are some of the things to experience while you are in Sri Lanka.

  • Amazing Beaches

    • Sri Lanka has some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. Well known as a paradise for surfers, there are various surfing spots along the coast for surfers of all levels. Some of the most famous surfing patches are in Arugam Bay, Unawatuna, Hikkaduwa, Weligama and Mirissa.

  • National Heritage

    • Sri Lanka has 8 UNESCO World Heritage sites, mostly religous while there are a couple of natural ones as well. The must-see sites are the city of Kandy with the Temple of the Tooth Relic, Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, Hortain Plains and Dambulla.

  • Hikes and Treks

    • Who wouldn't love to get a 360 degree view from the top of a mountain? For all you adventurers who just love spending time with nature, Sri Lanka offers a wide range of mountains and rocks to climb. Few of the most popular places for hiking and trekking are the Knuckles Mountain Range, Ella Rock, Adam's Peak, Pidurangala and Belihuloya.

  • Scenic Train Rides

    • If you ever visit Sri Lanka, it is a must to have a ride on the Kandy to Ella train. Some even say that this particular train ride is the most beautiful one in the world. Although the trains are quite slow, you can just hang out from the train doors and look at the endless range of mountains, trees and greenery.

  • Wildlife Tours

    • Sri Lanka has a wide range of national parks. If you are an animal lover, you can find just the right places to get into an open-top jeep and ride through jungles at the crack of dawn. Yala, Uda Walawe and Wilpattu are 3 of the most popular ones to visit.
